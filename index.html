Introduction

Work With Us helps you find motivation and happinies on your work!

Set up your goals and get start!

How it Works

The core of Work With Us is companionship. Set a timer to work with fellows in the villige. You will get rewards according to the time you work. Don't forget a moderate rest is also important!

Character

There are animals in various professionals working in the villige, come and be a part of them!

Summun

After getting salary from your work, you can summun animals to the villige.

Other

After midnignt,(12 a.m.) animals working in daytimes will not be working, while animals working at night will continue working. For example, animals working as a pilot will still be working.
